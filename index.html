Projects



I'm Dalton Banks, a master's student in Robotics at the University of Pennsylvania. I am interested in mechatronic systems for rehabilitation and accessibility, and I work as a research assistant in Dr. Michelle Johnson's Rehabilitation Robotics Lab.

haXbox

The haXbox lets users with special needs connect customized input devices to standard game systems. I developed this with Noam Eisen as a final project for ESE 350 (Embedded Systems), and we are continuing development with it. Take a look at the report for more details.
